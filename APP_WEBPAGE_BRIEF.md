# HundredOut App — Web Page Brief for Design Agent

Use this document to build the HundredOut app marketing/landing page. It covers content, copy direction, feature hierarchy, and design notes. All copy below is usable as-is or as a starting point — don't invent features not listed here.

---

## Brand Tone

Bold, confident, golf-native. Not corporate. Not "tech startup." Speaks to real golfers who bet with their buddies, care about their game, and want something built for how they actually play. Short sentences. Direct. A little attitude. Think: a golf brand that talks like a golfer.

Primary color: `#EE455F` (HundredOut red)
Secondary: dark navy/charcoal (`#1A2533`), white, light greys
Font feel: strong, semi-bold headers. Clean body copy.

## Core Features (Section: "Everything in one round")

Present as a feature grid or alternating left/right rows with app screenshots.

---

### 1. GPS Yardage
**Label:** Know the shot.
**Copy:** Real-time GPS distances to the front, center, and back of every green. No rangefinder required. Just open the app and play.

---

### 2. Digital Scorecard
**Label:** Keep it honest.
**Copy:** Clean, fast scoring for solo rounds or full groups. Track strokes, putts, fairways, and GIR. Your round, always with you.

---

### 3. Dots (Betting)
**Label:** Make it interesting.
**Copy:** The classic Dots game — built in. Automatic match tracking, leaderboard, and dot tallies updated hole by hole. Set your bet, play your round, settle up at the 19th.

Supports: Nassau, Skins, Birdies, Presses, custom formats.

**Design note:** Show the in-game leaderboard screen. This is a key differentiator — give it visual weight.

---

### 4. Side Juice (Side Bets)
**Label:** Extra action, anytime.
**Copy:** Create side bets mid-round between any two players. Stroke handicap, auto-press when you go down, real-time tracking. Side Juice lives inside the app — no Venmo math required at the end.

---

### 5. Challenges
**Label:** Call your shot.
**Copy:** Send stroke challenges to anyone in your crew. Pick the hole, set the terms, settle it live. Challenges stack — keep as many going as you want.

---

### 6. Crews & Social Feed
**Label:** Your golf crew, in one place.
**Copy:** Build your crew, share rounds to the feed, react to shots, and see who's out playing right now. The Haps feed is where your golf life lives — fired-away posts, round recaps, hole-in-ones, the whole thing.

**Design note:** Show the Haps feed and/or Active Users strip. This is the social hook — make it feel alive.

---

### 7. Trophies & HUNNIDs
**Label:** Every round pays off.
**Copy:** Earn HUNNIDs (our in-app currency) for every score you post — birdies, eagles, full 18-hole rounds. Unlock trophies for chaos moments, hidden achievements, and crew milestones. The more you play, the more you rack up.

**HUNNIDs earn rates (for copy reference, do not display as a table):**
Fire Away +25 · Birdie +50 · Eagle +75 · Hole in One +100 · Albatross +100 · Par +5

## Notes for the Web Agent

- This is a mobile-first product. The page itself should be designed mobile-first.
- The primary CTA is app download. Everything else supports that.
- Do not invent features not listed above (e.g., swing analysis, shot tracking, handicap index integration — these don't exist in the current app).
- The "HUNNIDs" name is intentional and branded — always spelled in all-caps.
- "Haps" is the name of the social feed — use it that way.
- "FMB" (Fire My Ball) is the center nav button — this is internal terminology; don't use it on the marketing page.
- "Dots" is the betting game — always capitalize it.
- "Side Juice" is the side bets feature — always two words, capitalized.
- The app is available on both iOS and Android.
- Keep all price references accurate: Crew $49.99/yr, Core Crew $99.99/yr.
