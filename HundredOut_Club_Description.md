# HundredOut Club — Full Description

## What Is the HundredOut Club?

The HundredOut Club is HundredOut's voluntary supporter program — a way for players who love the app to ride with us. There are no feature gates, no paywalls, and no pay-to-win mechanics. Every feature in HundredOut is free to every player. The Club is for people who want to be part of something bigger and support what we're building.

There are two tiers: **HundredOut Crew** ($49.99/year) and **HundredOut Core Crew** ($99.99/year).

---

## HundredOut Crew — $49.99/year

### The Idea
The Crew tier is for players who are all-in on HundredOut and want the gear to show for it. You get real physical product shipped to you, an ad-free experience, and access to member pricing on every drop we make.

### YOUR GEAR
- **HundredOut original logo hat** — classic HundredOut branding, shipped to your door
- **HundredOut metal ball marker** — quality metal, HundredOut logo

### MEMBERSHIP PERKS
- **Ad-free experience** — no ads, ever, as long as you're a member
- **Member pricing on every drop** — every future product release, you pay less
- **Early access before anything goes public** — drops, features, announcements — you get them first
- **Redeemable HUNNIDs earned** — HUNNIDs are the in-app currency earned by playing (birdies, eagles, pars, fire aways). Members can redeem them when the HundredOut marketplace launches.

### Price
$49.99/year — billed annually through the App Store.

---

## HundredOut Core Crew — $99.99/year

### The Idea
Core Crew is the founding member tier. It's for the people who were there from the beginning — players who want the exclusive gear, the identity, and the status that comes with being in from day one. The Core Crew hat is dated annually, meaning your 2026 hat will always be a 2026 hat. That's a founding member piece.

### YOUR GEAR
- **Exclusive 2026 Core Crew hat — annual edition** — dated with the year, exclusive to Core Crew members. Not available anywhere else. Each year's edition is unique and only available during that membership year.
- **Core Crew metal ball marker** — distinct Core Crew branding, separate from the standard HundredOut ball marker
- **Core Crew in-app badge** — a unique badge that appears on your profile and in rounds. Other players can see it. It marks you as Core Crew. The 2026 badge is the founding member badge — it will always show you were there from the start.

### MEMBERSHIP PERKS
- **Ad-free experience** — same as Crew tier, no ads ever
- **First dibs on every drop we make** — before Crew members, before the public
- **VIP access — events + member pricing** — VIP entry to HundredOut events and the best pricing on every product we release
- **+100 HUNNIDs on join — Core Crew exclusive** — a one-time 100 HUNNID welcome bonus credited to your in-app balance when you join

### The Badge
The Core Crew badge is a circular navy and red mark featuring the number 100, a crown, and "CC 2026." It appears on your profile and is visible to other players during rounds and in the social feed. The 2026 badge is the founding edition — members who join in 2026 will always have it. Future years will have their own annual badge.

## The Founding Member Angle

Anyone who joins Core Crew in 2026 is a founding member. The hat is dated 2026. The badge says CC 2026. In 2027 and beyond, when new members join, they'll see that you were there from the beginning. The "Core Crew since '26" status is permanent — it lives on your profile forever.

This is not a loyalty points program. This is identity. You're part of the crew that built this thing from the ground up.

## HUNNIDs — The In-App Currency

HUNNIDs are earned by playing:
- Fire away (start a round): +25
- Par: +5
- Birdie: +50
- Eagle: +75
- Albatross or hole-in-one: +100

HUNNIDs accumulate in your profile balance. The HundredOut marketplace — where HUNNIDs can be redeemed for products and perks — is coming. Crew and Core Crew members will have redemption access when it launches.

Core Crew members receive a **+100 HUNNID welcome bonus** on join, on top of whatever they earn through play.

## How to Join

The HundredOut Club is available inside the HundredOut app:
- Tap the club badge in the top of the Home screen
- Navigate to your profile → Support the App
- Available on iOS via the App Store (in-app purchase)
- Android support: coming soon
